What are the origins of Unplugged Life?

Just like many other podcasters we started in the dinning room of our apartment. We were discussing how expensive everything has gotten and how we could become self-sufficient. Once we figured out what worked for us we wanted to share that knowledge with others, so the podcast Unplugged Life was born.

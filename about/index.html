Our Mission - we want to help you live a life that allows you to explore the world more instead of being tired down financially to your home.

Welcome to "Unplugged Life," the podcast that's here to guide you on your journey towards a more eco-friendly and sustainable life, all while keeping you entertained and engaged. I'm your host, Maya Greenfield, and I'm not your typical sustainability guru. I believe that living sustainably doesn't mean giving up all of life's pleasures; it's about making mindful choices that benefit both you and the planet. With my relatable and down-to-earth personality, I'll be sharing personal anecdotes and real-life experiences that will make you feel like you're chatting with an old friend. Whether you're a seasoned environmental enthusiast or just dipping your toes into the world of sustainability, I'm here to show you that every small step counts and that we're all in this together.

What sets "Unplugged Life" apart is my unique blend of subject matter expertise and quirky sense of humor. Each episode will dive deep into a specific aspect of sustainable living, from zero-waste tips and ethical fashion to renewable energy and conscious consumerism. But don't worry, I'll be tackling these topics with a lighthearted approach and a sprinkle of humor. Who said learning about carbon footprints and composting had to be dull? So, if you're looking for a podcast that not only offers valuable insights into sustainable living but also leaves you with a smile on your face, tune in to "Unplugged Life." Together, we'll navigate the twists and turns of this eco-conscious journey while having a good laugh along the way.
